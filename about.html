Hi, I'm Mattia!

I'm currently traveling the world with a film camera in hand, documenting the extreme, the obscure, and the culturally electric — from underground rituals to chaotic street festivals, the kinds of moments that don't make headlines but leave a mark.

I shoot exclusively on analog because I want to capture things as they are: raw, imperfect, and real. There's something grounding in slowing down the process in a world that's constantly speeding up.

Before this chapter, I spent the past several years deep in tech — building, investing, and experimenting in San Francisco.

I worked on AI weather models to enable weather modification and control — part of a broader effort to increase rainfall and mitigate natural disasters.

I've managed over $150M venture capital and invested LP's funds in companies like OpenAI, Anduril, SpaceX, SSI, xAI, Databricks, Plaid, Perplexity, and over 200 seed-stage YC startups.

A little further back, I co-founded a payments company, consulted for large public Italian firms, and ran a pickleball and tennis school, where I coached for a while.

Now I'm stepping back from the screen and into the world — chasing fire, noise, color, and chaos. This site is where I share what I find.
